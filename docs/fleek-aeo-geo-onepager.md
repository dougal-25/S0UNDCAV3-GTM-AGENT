# Making Fleek the answer — an AEO/GEO growth plan on a napkin

*A POV artifact for the Fleek Organic Growth Lead (SEO & AI Search) role. Written to be
argued with, not just read. Measured on **buyers from organic + AI search**, not traffic.*

**Thesis.** Win where resellers actually decide: **own** the data-backed pages only Fleek
can build, and **earn** our way into the sources LLMs cite. Most "AEO" is snake oil — no
`llms.txt`, no audit theatre, no AI-slop. The levers that move the number are earned
mentions, real marketplace data, and rigorous measurement.

---

## The engine (is it only Reddit? No.)

Five surfaces, full intent curve. Reddit is one box in *Earn* — not the strategy.

```
        OWN  ───────────────►  EARN  ───────────────►  DEFEND
  (website as product)     (where LLMs cite)        (entity + reputation)
  • programmatic pages     • Reddit, YouTube         • Trustpilot velocity
    brand×category×era,     • review sites, roundups  • "is Fleek legit" surface
    per-country duty,       • press / digital PR      • brand-entity / knowledge
    vendor pages           • 1,000+ creators + UGC
  • hub-and-spoke clusters
  • help-centre from CX     won via genuine help,     defends the trust query
  • landing pages          not posts about us         that converts or kills us
        │                         │                         │
        └─────────────►  FOUNDATION (hygiene): schema · sitemaps · product
                          metadata · site speed — ship what I can, scope eng for the rest
        └─────────────►  MEASURE: SOV + citation monitoring · "how did you hear
                          about us?" · zero-click attribution → WBR → stage-gated budget
```

- **Own** wins mid/long-tail and *converts* intent. Programmatic pages survive Google's
  2026 quality updates **only when backed by real, differentiated data** (the Wise / Zillow
  / G2 pattern) — Fleek's live inventory, price ranges, sell-through and vendor coverage are
  exactly that moat, and competitors can't fake them.
- **Earn** wins the **head questions own pages can't** ("best wholesale vintage supplier",
  "is Fleek legit"). The large majority of AI citations are earned media (Reddit, YouTube,
  reviews, news) — so we get *mentioned* in the cited ecosystem rather than publishing about
  ourselves.
- **Defend** because "is Fleek legit" is a buy/don't-buy query, and LLMs lean on Trustpilot
  and Reddit to answer it.

## Operating principles (why I won't waste the budget)

1. **Earned > owned** for head questions — we win them by being cited, not by self-publishing.
2. **Data is the citation lever.** The peer-reviewed GEO study (Aggarwal et al., ACM KDD
   2024) shows adding **statistics, quotes, cited sources and fluent/authoritative prose**
   lifts LLM citation ~30–40%; keyword-stuffing *hurts*. Fleek's marketplace data is the
   unfair advantage.
3. **Real data or no page.** Programmatic scale only on genuine marketplace differentiation —
   never thin template permutations.
4. **Every initiative ships with a hypothesis, a baseline/holdout, and a kill-or-scale gate.**
5. **No snake oil:** schema and site speed are table-stakes hygiene, not the strategy; skip
   `llms.txt` and audit theatre; never ship purely AI-generated content.

## Phase-2 bets — tested, not assumed

Both are experiments with a baseline and a gate, not commitments.

- **Reseller resale events (top/exclusive resellers).** *Hypothesis:* IRL events manufacture
  citable earned media (press, creator recaps, Reddit/YouTube threads), deepen the creator
  roster, and produce UGC. *Measure:* citation + branded-search lift in the 30 days post-event
  vs a no-event window; attributed signups. *Gate:* scale only if it beats creator payouts on
  SOV / branded-search per £.
- **Fleek podcast / educational talks.** *Hypothesis:* long-form, expert, data-rich content is
  exactly what LLMs cite (transcripts parse cleanly) and it builds topical authority + creator
  relationships. *Measure:* citations/mentions generated, YouTube pickup, assisted signups.
  *Gate:* continue only if cited or driving attributed buyers. *Start lean* — recorded reseller
  AMAs, not a studio show — to keep production cost honest against payoff.
