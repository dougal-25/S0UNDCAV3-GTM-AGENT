## Problem (the ache, in their words)

> "The music's done. Now you need a cover, a square, a story, a banner — all by
> Friday, and a designer wants £150 for a song forty people will hear."

Every release needs visuals. Designers are slow and expensive; Canva is a
time-sink; stock looks like stock. So the artwork is the part everyone dreads.

## How it works (3 steps)

1. **Tell it about your release** — track, vibe, or just a title.
2. **Pick a style** — named looks like *Etchings* (more coming).
3. **Get a full pack** — cover, single/EP art, social squares, flyer — ready to post.
